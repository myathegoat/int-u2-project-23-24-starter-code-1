Solutions for gun violence

Our mission is to end the cycle of gun violence. We do this through reduction strategy. This combines collective accountability and provison of social services to deliver antiviolence messages to street groups at high risk for involvement in shootings. Street groups could also collaborate with law enforcement to implore group members to stop the violence. The goal is to better broadcast community-wide standards against violence, provide services to group members, and use targeted enforcement to deter violence.
